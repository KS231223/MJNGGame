TILE:
Power tiles:
- winds are represented by suit: wind, number = 1,2,3,4 (dong, nan, xi, bei)
- same for big tiles, suit = power, number = 1,2,3 (hong zhong, fa, bai ban)

point tiles:
- flowers are just suit = flowers, number = 1,2,3,4
- animals are suit = animals, number = 1,2,3,4 (chicken, centipede, cat, mouse)

normal tiles:
- self explanatory, suit = ball,stick,wan, number = 1-9
